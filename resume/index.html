Professional Summary

I am a game designer and developer with a strong background in large-scale commercial game production, most notably at Ubisoft RedLynx. My work has spanned mobile and console projects, onboarding and retention systems, meta-design, and collaboration across multidisciplinary teams.

Over time, my priorities have shifted toward applying these skills outside of pure entertainment. I am increasingly focused on building tools, systems, and experiences that align with ethical values — particularly animal rights, transparency, and education.

This website, and my current career direction, represent an intentional move toward technology that informs, empowers, and contributes positively beyond traditional commercial products.
